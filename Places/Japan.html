The superb Japanese cuisine

Japanese culture places a high value on food, and when visiting the nation, you can enjoy a wide variety of the many delectable foods that make up Japanese cuisine.

Thrilling experiences

The alternatives for thrill-seekers are endless, from snowboarding or skiing in Hokkaido or Nagano to cycling along the Seto Inland Sea, gaining a glimpse of Mount Fuji from the rollercoasters of FujiQ Highlands, or sandboarding in Tottori, Japan's desert.

Pristine natural scenery

It goes without saying that the nation's natural beauty is the main incentive for travel. Japan is a nature lover's heaven in many aspects.
